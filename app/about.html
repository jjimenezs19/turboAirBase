About Us

Eluvio is a quest to enable the
Internet for video.

We use the philosophies of decentralized design and content-centric networking, and
we harness breakthroughs in machine learning, blockchain security, advanced
cryptography, and low cost compute to achieve efficiency and simplicity.

Several of us have spent our careers dealing with the challenges of current
architectures and see now an opportunity to change the Internet for the better, for
good, and an economic imperative given the imbalances in the content distribution
chain working against Content Publishers, Sponsors and Viewers.

Many will recognize that Eluvio is the “rest of the story” following our creation of the first
Content transport for the Internet fifteen years ago. Our team is humble but equipped
with intellectual passion and a powerhouse of technical depth. In Latin, “fluvio” is a
flood. Hence our ambition.

Michelle Munson

CEO/Founder

Michelle Munson is the co-founder and CEO of Eluvio. She previously founded Aspera and led the company as CEO for 13 years including through acquisition by IBM in 2014. She co-invented the Aspera FASP transport technology used widely for high-speed media file transfer. She was the 2016 Charles Swartz awardee and is a SMPTE Fellow. Michelle has dual B.Sc. degrees in Electrical Engineering and in Physics from Kansas State University and was a Goldwater Scholar for achievement in Science and Mathematics, and later a Fulbright Scholar at Cambridge University where she received a masters in Computer Science.

Serban Simu

President/Founder

Serban is a technology innovator and hands-on engineering leader in all things "data". Serban has a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Universitatea Politehnica Bucuresti in Bucharest, Romania, and a Masters degree in Telecommunication Networks from Ecole Nationale Superieure des Telecommunications where he finished first in his class. He came to the United States in the late 1990’s to join Cisco Content Networking group, as a lead software engineer at Digital Fountain, and founded Aspera with Michelle Munson in 2004 where he led technology and product development for 13 years.

Marc Lusinchi

MMarc is an expert in media workflow design and automation and in the application of blockchain security to these areas. At Aspera, he led the development of the Orchestrator offering and was very involved in the delivery of media automation services. Prior to Aspera, Marc worked for Accenture, where he led the implementation of numerous complex information systems.

Lukas Anliker

Lukas is a deeply experienced software architect with a strong technical background in telecommunication networks, cloud computing and distributed systems. After graduating from EPFL Lausanne with a Masters degree in telecommunication systems, he spent several years in Silicon Valley as a Software Developer for major ISPs. He co-founded Castify Networks, a creator of content delivery software solutions, and lead its engineering team, and eventually joined Aspera and led its European engineering team and a multitude of technical innovations there in cloud systems integration and multicast transfer mechanisms for ten years.

Michael Parker

Michael is an expert software architect with deep experience in databases, web applications, and now video. At Eluvio he is deeply involved in much of the content and application pipeline (and reads a lot of SMPTE standards). Before Eluvio he created the Aspera Faspex and Console products and led development of these products for 10 years.

Bill Jones

Bill Jones has over 25 years experience in large-scale network design and operations and heads up all things “Internet Protocol” at Eluvio.

John Heaton

John Heaton heads up infrastructure and systems at Eluvio. Prior to Eluvio, John has held positions as a Senior Solutions Engineer for Google Cloud and was the Worldwide Director of Sales Engineering for Aspera, responsible for deployments across all verticals. John holds a Bachelor or Science in Computer Science from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC).

Youliang Yu

Youliang holds a Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics from Kansas State University with a focus on numerical computation of quantum phenomena in molecular ionization. At Eluvio, Youliang leads a team of data scientists/engineers to solve problems in digital media--video/audio/text--classification, search, time-series forecasting, and recommender systems. His strengths include machine learning methods, mathematical modeling, optimization, and rapid prototype of real-world applications applying these
methods. He holds a Kaggle Master title for his consistent and stellar competition results on Kaggle.

Paul O'Leary

Paul O'Leary is an experienced technology leader with over 25 years of industry experience, including more than 15 at the executive leadership level. He most recently was CTO and VP Engineering at Kissmetrics, where he managed all technology, engineering and operations aspects of their large-scale, cloud-based analytics and data processing infrastructure. Paul has extensive hands-on technology experience in analytics, data processing, application infrastructure, security, and advanced cryptography and has brought all of these to Elvio's innovative architecture. Previous technology leadership positions include Concurrent (Driven Inc.), Quantivo (where he was co-founder), Privia and Broadbase.
